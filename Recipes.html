Matcha Recipes

Discover My Favorite Matcha Recipes

Here are some of my favorite iced matcha recipes that I’ve made and absolutely loved! There are so many creative ways to enjoy matcha, but I’ve selected a few of my top picks for you to try. Each one is refreshing, delicious, and perfect for matcha lovers.

1- Whisk 4 g matcha powder with 50 ml warm water until smooth.
2- Fill a glass with ice cubes.
3- Pour in 1 cup cold milk.
4- Add the whisked matcha over the milk.
5- Sweeten with honey or syrup if desired.

1- Whisk 4 g matcha powder with 50 ml warm water.
2- Fill a glass with ice and pour 1 cup cold milk.
3- Add whisked matcha on top.
4- Make vanilla foam: whip 2 tbsp heavy cream with 1 tsp vanilla syrup.
5- Spoon foam over the drink and drizzle extra syrup.

1- Mix 1 tsp ground cinnamon, 1 tsp brown sugar, and a splash of warm water for syrup.
2- Whisk 4 g matcha powder with 50 ml warm water.
3- In a glass, add cinnamon syrup, ice cubes, and 1 cup cold milk.
4- Pour whisked matcha over milk.
5- Top with whipped cream and sprinkle cinnamon.

1- Whisk 8 g matcha powder with 100 ml warm water until smooth.
2- Use most of the whisked matcha to prepare a classic iced matcha latte: fill a glass with ice, pour 1 cup cold milk, then add the matcha.
3- Take the remaining matcha and mix it with 2 tbsp heavy cream to create a light matcha foam.
4- Spoon the matcha foam on top of the latte.
5- Sprinkle extra matcha powder for garnish and enjoy!

1- Whisk 4 g matcha powder with 50 ml warm water.
2- Fill a glass with ice and pour 1 cup cold milk.
3- Add whisked matcha on top.
4- Make strawberry foam: whip 2 tbsp heavy cream with 1 tbsp strawberry puree.
5- Spoon foam over the drink.

1- Blend ½ cup mango chunks with 1 tbsp honey until smooth.
2- Whisk 4 g matcha powder with 50 ml warm water.
3- In a glass, add mango puree, ice cubes, and 1 cup cold milk.
4- Pour whisked matcha on top.
5- Finish with a light drizzle of mango puree for extra flavor.
